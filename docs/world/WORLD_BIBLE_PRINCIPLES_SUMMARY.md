# World Bible Principles Summary

This summary captures the world principles that matter for product and contract work in v1.

## Core world law
- repeated meaningful action reshapes reality
- growth is witnessed, not abstract
- restoration matters as much as conquest
- civilian, domestic, scholarly, and mercantile fantasies matter as much as martial ones

## Product translation
- habit maps to `Vow`
- streak maps to `Chain`
- recovery maps to `Embers` and `Rekindling`
- the game layer rewards behavior; it does not replace behavior

## v1 world limits
- launch region is Cyrane / Dawnmarch only
- one district restoration arc
- one 7-day starter arc
- one 30-day Chapter One shell
- world flavor should personalize the experience without creating separate mechanical rule systems per identity

## Tone rule
Use plain English for core utility flows and lightly mythic framing for flavor layers. Never let world language obscure setup, logging, subscription, permission, or recovery behavior.
