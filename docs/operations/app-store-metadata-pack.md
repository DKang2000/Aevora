## Full Description
Aevora helps you build a small set of daily vows and see your real progress reflected inside a warm fantasy world.

Start by choosing a fantasy identity, accept or edit a focused set of daily vows, and log your progress in one or two taps. As you follow through, you earn Resonance, Gold, and visible world restoration across a seven-day starter arc and the first chapter beyond it.

Launch features include:
- quick manual vow logging
- three habit types: yes/no, count, and duration
- Chains, Embers, and Rekindling recovery
- a portrait-first Today flow with world, hearth, quest, and inventory surfaces
- widgets and Live Activities
- optional HealthKit verification for narrow premium inputs
- guest mode plus Sign in with Apple continuity

Premium expands breadth, customization, and witness surfaces. It never replaces the core behavior loop.
