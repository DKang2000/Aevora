# Privacy Policy Draft

## Purpose
Explain the data Aevora processes for launch in plain language.

## Summary
Aevora stores the information needed to power your habit progress, account continuity, subscriptions, widgets, notifications, and optional HealthKit verification. We keep manual logging primary and only use HealthKit for the narrow domains you explicitly authorize.

## Data We Collect
- account identifiers for guest mode or Sign in with Apple continuity
- vow definitions, completion history, progression state, and inventory state
- subscription and restore state
- notification preferences and delivery planning state
- analytics events that follow the repo’s privacy-redaction rules
- optional HealthKit-derived completion signals for supported domains only

## Data We Do Not Collect
- freeform note text in analytics
- raw HealthKit payloads in analytics
- mental-health judgments or punitive labels for missed days

## How We Use Data
- provide the core habit and progression experience
- keep your local and backend state in sync
- restore entitlements and support account continuity
- measure onboarding, retention, monetization, and economy health
- diagnose product and support issues through support-safe audit and reporting paths

## Sharing
Launch scope is single-player first. We do not provide a public social feed or share your progress publicly by default.

## Account Export and Deletion
- A support-safe export can be prepared before deletion.
- Local account reset on device clears local state for that installation.
- Backend account deletion/export flows are governed by the account lifecycle docs and support process.
