# Launch FAQ and Support Macros

## FAQ
### I restored my purchase but my progress did not come back.
Restore rehydrates subscription state and breadth. It does not recreate local progress that was removed from the device.

### Notifications are not showing up.
Check whether iOS notifications are disabled for Aevora. Manual logging still works fully in-app even if notifications remain off.

### HealthKit is not importing my activity.
HealthKit is optional and narrow at launch. Manual logging remains primary, and duplicate verified rewards are intentionally suppressed.

### I used guest mode and switched devices.
Guest mode is local-first. Link your account with Sign in with Apple if you want server-backed continuity.

### How do I delete my account?
Start with an export preview, then review the delete flow in Profile. Linked or subscribed accounts may also require backend support deletion handling.

## Support Macros
### Restore Purchase
“Restore refreshes your subscription breadth, but it does not recreate local progress that was cleared from a device. If you were using guest mode only, please confirm whether that device state still exists locally.”

### Notifications
“Aevora keeps reminders intentionally sparse. If reminders stopped entirely, please check iOS notification permissions first. The app still supports full manual logging even with notifications off.”

### HealthKit
“HealthKit support is narrow at launch and additive only. Please confirm Aevora still has HealthKit authorization and note that manual logging remains the primary path.”

### Account Linking
“Guest progress is local-first. To preserve continuity beyond one device, link with Sign in with Apple before clearing local state.”

### Export and Delete
“Please prepare an export before deletion so the current account state is visible. Local reset and backend deletion are related but not identical actions.”
