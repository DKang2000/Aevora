# Terms of Service Draft

## Scope
Aevora is a single-player, subscription-supported iPhone app intended to help users build daily habits through a fantasy progression framework.

## Launch Terms Summary
- You may use Aevora through guest mode or a linked Sign in with Apple account.
- Premium expands breadth and witness surfaces; it does not auto-complete vows or bypass the behavior loop.
- Subscription restore, downgrade, and expiration behavior follow the canonical entitlement matrix.
- You remain responsible for the information you enter manually.
- Optional HealthKit verification is additive and requires your explicit permission.

## Service Availability
We aim to provide a reliable local-first experience, but some services depend on backend availability, Apple services, and platform permissions.

## Account and Deletion
- You may request export and deletion according to the documented account lifecycle.
- Deletion of an account may remove access to server-backed continuity for that account.

## Non-goals
These draft terms do not replace final legal review before public launch.
